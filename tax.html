Standard Tax Documents - Check List (Avoid SURE audit. Report all your income.)

  1. Change(s) filing status - S, MFJ, MFS
  2. Change(s) of your dependent status
  3. Change of your mailing address
  4. Income group
    • Form W-2 for salaries, wages, tips, etc
    • Form 1099 - taxable interest income
    • Form 1099 - ordinary and qualified div
    • Form 1099G - taxable state refund
    • Alimony received
    • Form 1099 - business/other income
    • Capital gain/loss from sale of stock
    • Other gain/loss from sales of business
    • All Forms W/1099-R, G, SSA, etc
    • Rental or farm income
    • Other: winnings, cancelled debts, wtc.
    * Bring all tax related papers and let us determine which is tax related.
  5. Deductions group
    • Educator expenses
    • Business expenses - summarized
    • Health savings account
    • Moving expenses
    • Self employed health insurance
    • Penalty for early withdrawal of savings
    • Alimony paid/child care
    • Student loan interest/bat debts
    • Tuition fees form 1098 T - from school
    • Other deductions like church/charities
